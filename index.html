THE SCIENCE

Ditch the routine.
Embrace the cycle

Traditional study routines are linear and fragile. One missed day can derail your entire plan. Fostern uses AI-driven study cycles based on educational psychology adapting to how you actually learn, not how you think you should.

Research from institutions like MIT and Stanford shows that interleaved, spaced practice outperforms blocked studying in every measurable way. Fostern builds this science directly into your study flow.

Adaptive, not rigid

A study cycle adjusts to your pace and performance. Fixed routines assume every day is the same — your brain isn't.

Spaced repetition built-in

Cycling through subjects at intervals leverages the spacing effect — proven by cognitive science to boost long-term retention by up to 200%.

Prevents burnout

Fixed schedules create pressure and guilt when missed. Cycles are resilient — skip a day, and the system continues seamlessly.

Real progress, not illusion

Routines feel productive but often lead to passive re-reading. Cycles force active recall and interleaving — the two most effective learning strategies known.

Gamified Learning

We treat your learning journey like a game.

Every session earns XP. Every milestone unlocks achievements. Fostern saves your entire progress — streaks, scores, mastered cards — so you never lose momentum. Your study data persists across all devices. Pick up exactly where you left off with AI that remembers everything you've learned and everything you still need to conquer.
